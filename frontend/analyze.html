Analysis Guidelines

1

Ensure Proper Lighting

Use natural, diffused light. Avoid harsh shadows or direct flash that might obscure details.

2

Macro Focus

Capture the lesion from 10-15cm away. The focus should be sharp on the skin texture.

3

Clear Surroundings

Remove any hair, bandages, or clothing that might obstruct the view of the lesion edges.
